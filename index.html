When the script is activated you will recieve the text you highlight in a paragraph tag below. You can also click on certain text elements to easily highlight the entire element.
Also, if you click on the text you just highlighted or on an element that does not have the 'data-readable' attribute your previous text will be 'unselected'.

Try it out by setting the button to 'Activated'

  • Try to click on me, I will be highlighted with only one click
  • I won't be highlighted with only one click, as I don't have a 'data-readable' attribute
  • I can also be clicked to be highlighted
